Miscellaneous Works

  • The Age of the AI Agent (June 2025)

    An essay responding to and extending Brian Rotman's book 'Becoming Beside Ourselves.' Investigates the validity of the computer in mathematics, and its role as a physical copy of the mathematical agent and its connection to AI.

  • The Implications of the Holy Ghost in Turing's Machine (May 2025)

    Another essay responding to a book by Brian Rotman, this time 'Ad Infinitum.' Explores the assumption of infinity in mathematics, and this assumption's trickle-down effects on science and mathematics social relations to other fields in academia.

  • Sac FC Instagram

    An Instagram page dedicated to following the intramural soccer team I started at UCSB. The captions provided a weekly comedic writing exercise if nothing else. No longer managed by me as of June 2025.

  • Daily Nexus Article

    An article I wrote about the UCSB men's soccer goalkeeper my freshman year as a data visualization exercise. Oddly enough, he requested it in pdf form to aid in his application for a work Visa or something of the sort (he's German).
