Projects

  • Statistics Education (Jan-June 2025)

    Analyzed survey data and text data on UCSB PSTAT courses 10 and 120B under the instruction and project supervision of Dr. Uma Ravat. Data included but was not limited to post lecture free responses, demographics, tech experience, and course skill improvement. Used text analysis and linear regression, all done in R, and data and project are unavailable due to privacy restrictions.

  • ISI BUDS mHealth Symposium (June-Aug 2024)

    Under the guidance of Dr. Babak Shahbaba of UCI, my ISI-BUDS final project which analyzed mHealth data from the ALLofUS dataset. Looked at disparities in cardiovascular diseases amongst minorities and women. Imputed data with kNN and MICE, as well as used FDA visualizations and linear and logistic regression models. Used R.

  • Wikipedia SNA

    In my final project for a computational statistics course, two partners and myself practiced social network analysis on SNAP Wikipedia articles about animals. We measured their connections to the rest of Wikipedia, each other, and measures of site popularity. Used R and iGraph.

  • Random Number Generation

    In an independent midterm project for the same computational statistics course, I practiced inversion sampling, rejection sampling, and transformation sampling on univariate distributions. Used R.
